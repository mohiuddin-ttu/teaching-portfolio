Research

Job Market Paper

Consumer Demand and Household Welfare under Large Energy Price Shocks

Studies oil-price shocks, household consumption smoothing, and welfare using IV-GMM, local projections, Bayesian VARs, and structural interpretation.

Wildfire Shocks and Local Employment Dynamics

Examines county-level labor-market adjustment to wildfire exposure using spatial panel methods, local projections, and regional heterogeneity analysis.

Technological Rivalry and the Reorganization of Strategic Trade Networks

Studies how AI-related technological exposure reshapes U.S. strategic trade concentration and dependence on China-linked supply networks.

Medicare Eligibility and Household Financial Outcomes

Uses a fuzzy regression discontinuity design around age 65 to estimate the effect of Medicare eligibility on utilization, out-of-pocket spending, debt, labor supply, and retirement.
